HI, I'M SNEHA BANG.

An Explorer of Human-Centric Design
My journey into design started with a humble project: reimagining a local bakery's brand. That small task opened my eyes to the profound impact design can have on how people perceive and interact with the world around them. Since then, I’ve been on a mission to dig deeper, uncovering the hidden needs and motivations of users to create solutions that truly resonate.

Shaping Experiences with User-Centered Lens
With a Master’s in Human-Computer Interaction from Michigan State University, I’ve had the privilege to blend the art and science of design. I don’t just design interfaces; I craft experiences that feel natural and empowering. Every project, whether in EdTech, healthcare, or beyond, is a new puzzle to solve—how to make technology intuitive, accessible, and a joy to use. It’s about more than just usability; it’s about making users feel confident and in control.

Design as a Narrative
To me, design is storytelling. Each button, color, and interaction is a word in a narrative that guides users effortlessly through their journey. I thrive on transforming complex data into clear, engaging interfaces, and translating insights into designs that speak directly to users’ needs. My role isn’t just to create beautiful visuals; it’s to be a voice for the user, ensuring that every decision made enhances the overall experience.

Driven by Curiosity and a Desire to Share
Continuous growth drives me—both personally and professionally. I’m always exploring new tools, technologies, and approaches to improve my craft. Sharing what I’ve learned through mentoring is something I’m passionate about because I believe that great design is rooted in collaboration and shared knowledge.

PORTFOLIO

Design thinking isn’t just a method; it's how I approach every project - blending creativity with strategy, empathy with data, and vision with practicality.

At the core of my design philosophy lies the principle of design thinking—a structured yet flexible process that puts the user first. It’s more than a framework; it’s a mindset that encourages empathy, creativity, and collaboration to create solutions that are not only functional but also meaningful.

Where Creativity Takes a Breath: My Life Beyond Design

Outside of design, I find balance and inspiration in a few other passions. Whether it’s the rhythm of a badminton rally, the peaceful flow of swimming, or the joy of dancing, these activities give me space to unwind and recharge.

I’m always eager to explore how I can bring value to your design and development needs. Let’s connect and create something amazing together!
